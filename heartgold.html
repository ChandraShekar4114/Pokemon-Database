About the Game

Pokémon HeartGold Version and Pokémon SoulSilver Version are paired Generation IV remakes of the Generation II games Pokémon Gold and Silver. Much like how Pokémon FireRed and LeafGreen revisited the Generation I story of Kanto, HeartGold and SoulSilver retell the story of Johto, with the player's starting town being New Bark Town. While the games feature several expansions in key areas, the overall plot follows the same direction as the original Gold and Silver. Some aspects exclusive to Crystal are also included. Like how FireRed and LeafGreen could link up with Ruby, Sapphire, and Emerald to complete the Pokédex by trading regionally exclusive Pokémon, HeartGold and SoulSilver can link up with Diamond, Pearl, and Platinum to obtain Pokémon unavailable in Johto and Kanto (such as the Sinnoh starters) and vice versa.

How The Game Works?

Choose your Starter Pokemon

Every game provides the user with the three starter pokemon. Among which the user has to choose one that he thinks will match his battling style. Game provides the user with three types named fire, water and grass!

Build your team around your Starter

The team the user builds will determine the difficulty in battling. The key to build a team is to identify the type of pokemon available to capture and type weaknesses to take advantage. This website will provide you with the best team!

Fight Gyms Leaders and Elite Four

The main aim of the game is to defeat the gym leaders of the particular regions which will earn the users gym badges which will ultimately enable them to battle elite four of the region. There are usually eight gym leaders per region!

Become the Champion

Once you defeat all the gym leaders for the gym badges and getting through to the final chamber after defeating the leaders of elite four, you finnaly have the chance to defeat the champion and become the new monarch of that region!
